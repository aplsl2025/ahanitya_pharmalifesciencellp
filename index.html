About Us

Nitya Enterprises is an agriculture-focused enterprise engaged in the cultivation of processing-grade potatoes in Badaun District, Uttar Pradesh—one of India’s most suitable regions for chip-making potato varieties. We cultivate Chipsona Potato Series 1, Series 2, and Series 3 using controlled agronomic practices, quality seed material, and field-level monitoring to achieve parameters required by the potato chips and wafers industry.

Our cultivation is planned with a clear focus on high dry matter, low reducing sugars, uniform tuber size, and excellent frying quality. Each harvest lot is evaluated against industry benchmarks for specific gravity, moisture, chip colour, and oil absorption, ensuring consistent performance during frying and processing. This results in light-coloured, crisp chips with high recovery and controlled oil uptake.
